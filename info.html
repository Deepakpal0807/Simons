"Simon is a fun and challenging memory game that tests your ability to remember and repeat sequences of colors and sounds. To play Simon, you'll need the Simon game device or app. The game consists of four colorful buttons - red, green, blue, and yellow. The objective is to follow the pattern set by the game, which starts with a single button lighting up and making a sound. Your task is to watch and listen carefully, then repeat the exact sequence by pressing the buttons in the same order. As you progress, the game will add one more step to the sequence after each successful round. The game continues until you make a mistake. Your goal is to achieve the highest score possible by accurately replicating the increasingly complex patterns. Simon is a great game for improving memory and concentration, and it's perfect for solo play or challenging friends to beat your high score."
